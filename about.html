About LeeAnn

Healing starts when people feel heard, supported, and understood.

My Story

My passion for functional nutrition grew from personal experiences, helping loved ones through health struggles, and discovering how deeply nutrition impacts every area of wellness.

I believe true healing comes from addressing root causes instead of simply managing symptoms. Through compassionate support and personalized guidance, I help clients create sustainable changes that improve energy, digestion, hormone balance, sleep, and overall wellbeing.

Nourish You 365 was created to provide a safe and supportive place for individuals and families to pursue healing naturally.
